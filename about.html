About Marangi Paints





Marangi Paints is driven by three key values:
World-Class Technology,
Highly Qualified Professionals and
Commitment towards Environmental Care.

Today, Marangi Paints can proudly claim to have an
in-house production capacity for an entire range
of wall paints and coverings as well as associated
decorative and construction products.

The Aesthetics of an Elegant Infrastructure

The most advanced manufacturing units ensure product lines of international standards – in paints, resins and packaging.

Strategically located Sales Depots at Nairobi, Kisumu, Mombasa, Eldoret and Kajiado service the main regions of Kenya. A comprehensive network of dealers and a fleet of modern trucks assure timely delivery of products throughout the country.
